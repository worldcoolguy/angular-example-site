Bottle Gourd in Tangy Mixed Veggie Gravy
Yeild: 6 Servings

INGREDIENTS
  • 1/4 cup butternut squash
  • 1/4 cup broccoli
  • 1/2 cilantro
  • 1 cup raw mango
  • 1/4 garlic leaves
  • 3 cups bottlegourd
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup carrot
  • 1 inch ginger
  • green chillies
DRY ROAST INGREDIENTS
  • Dry red chillies
  • 1/4 cup cashews
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tsp channa dal
  • 1/2 tsp urad dal
  • 1/2 cup coconut
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp
  • Grind all the spices into coarse paste
  • Grid all veggies and green EXCEPT bottlegourd
  • Add 1 tsp oil in a pan and mix both pastes with bottlegourd. Let it cook for 30 mins on a medium flame with lid and stir occasionally
Tangy bottle gourd curry with mixed veggie gravy is ready!
